Research Areas

We work at the intersection of computer science and biomedicine, developing cutting-edge deep learning models and large language models for clinical impact.

🏥

AI-driven Intelligent Healthcare

Developing state-of-the-art AI algorithms, particularly deep learning and large language models, to enable disease detection, prognosis, and risk assessment across diverse clinical settings.

🧬

Bioinformatics

Building intelligent computational frameworks to decode gene regulatory networks, predict protein function and structure, and model complex biological systems with curiosity-driven AI agents.

🛡️

Ethical & Trustworthy AI

Investigating privacy, security, bias, fairness, toxicity, and the societal implications of AI in medicine to ensure clinical AI is responsible, interpretable, and aligned with human values.
